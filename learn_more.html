History of the Conflict

IT DIDNT START OCTOBER 7TH

The conflict in Gaza has roots in DECADES of territorial disputes, colonialism, and the geopolitical struggles of the region. Contrary to what some groups of people might try telling you, this isn't something that just started over the last year, instead this is the continuation of a 76 year history that knowing the basics of is crucial for understanding this conflict.
